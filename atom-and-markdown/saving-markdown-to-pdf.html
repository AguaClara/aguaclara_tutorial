Saving Markdown to PDF¶

  1. Open Atom, open the Markdown file that you want to convert, and open the formatted preview (Ctrl + Shift + M).

  2. Right click on the formatted preview and click on “Open in Browser”.

  3. Right click on the browser page and click on “Print…”

  4. Change the Destination to “Save as PDF”, then click Save/Print.

Now, save Interactive-Tutorial-1-Markdown.md as a PDF and put it in your aguaclara_tutorial repository folder.
